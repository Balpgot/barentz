About the festival

The coronavirus has made its own adjustments to people's lives. For most of them, these changes were very difficult. «Barents Connection» began with the desire of four girls, Alexandra Timofeeva, Daria Bolkhovskaya, Sonya Poguts and Anastasia Mostovaya, to support talented people and cultural institutions of the Barents region during the coronavirus. Now the «Barents Connection» team has grown so much that it covers 10 cities, namely Murmansk, Kirkenes, Arkhangelsk, Tromsø, Syktyvkar, Oslo, Alto, Petrozavodsk, St. Petersburg and Moscow. We are all united by one goal - to connect people of the Barents region in conditions of closed borders. «Barents Connection» is a space where you can challenge yourself, broaden your mind, communicate and even "walk" around Russia and Norway without leaving home. But what is more important, you can make new friends and not only preserve, but also strengthen the bond between people of the Barents region. Let's get acquainted and keep in touch with «Barents Connection»!
